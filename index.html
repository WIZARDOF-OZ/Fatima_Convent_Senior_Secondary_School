A Legacy Of Vision

[The Sistes of Our Lady Of Fatima]

The religious insititue of the Sisters of our Lady of Fatima, which has a Generalate in Pune was founded in 1893, in Bellary-Karnataka, by Magr, Francis Xavier Kroot, a Mill Hill Missionary from Holland.

His intense love and zeal to spread the Kingdom of God inspired him to work for all, especially for the liberation of the down trodden and particulary women.

The vision of our founder impels us to be educators in joy and hope to build a society of persons whop are caring and responsible. It is therefore, our privilege to take up the challenge educating the youth with the love and compassion of Jesus Christ, The Supreme Teacher. Our priority and focus is however, on educating the under- Privileged and especially the girl-child.

We endeavour to incalucate in our students the Gospel Value of love, sharing and caring, forgiveness, equality of labour and human life, in order that they may live simple and meaningful life, with a spirit of patriotism and be promoters of justice, peace and harmony as responsible citizen do.

Our Aim Is:-

  • To maintain academic excellence in and through curricular and co-curricular activities.

  • To help the students recognize and know their talents and nurture them for overall growth and progress of the students.

  • To enable the students to realize the benefits of self disicipline sportsmanship and poisitve thinking.

  • To enable in our students the awareness of the presence of God in all that we do; and whatever we have and possess is His gift to us.

  • To enable in our students to create and build just society, wherein; they be the instrument to effect that much needed change in society.

    • In order to make our vision a reality we commit ourselve to Innovative ideas and a new approach in our educational work. This is feasible with God's help and the collective efforts of all concerned: the management, the staff, the students and the parents
