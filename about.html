Fatima Convent School Fatima Nagar, Rangia, Assam was established on 15th January 2007. The School derives its name from the management it belongs: "Sisters of Our Lady of Fatima, Northeast Region" who have its main headquaters in Pune, Maharashtra and the regional headquaters in Cepti, Rangia, Assam

Our Story

General Information

The Sisters have taken "Our Lady of Fatima" - Mary, the Mother of Jesus, as their Patroness. In 1917, Mother Mary appeared to the three little shepherd children: Lucia , Franciso and Jacinta in Fatima, Portugal proclaiming the message of Prayer and Penance for the Peace in the break of the First World War. Following her message during her siz apparitions to the three little children form the month of May to October in the year 1917, they prayed and made penance and proclaimed to others to follow the same , soon after which eventually the world-war ended with the establishment of peace in the world. The Sister of Our Lady of Fatima by their presence and their various activities mainly through the educational activities, strive to work for the establishment of peace and harmony in and around the places of their work. The main purpose of Fatima Convent School is to import an all-round education(academic, physical, mortal, intellectual, social , physchological and spiritual) primarily to Catholics for whose education , this institution has been established and also to other children irrespective of religion, race or language in so far, as is consonant with the principles of Catholic Faith. The school follows the CBSE(Central Board of Secondary Education) course.
